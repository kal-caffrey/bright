Looking for Customer-focused Professionals!

Through our extensive storage tank farms inside Rotterdam terminals and oil tanker ships navigating the inland waterways and coastal seas of European Russia, 1/2 LOGISTICS BV. Tank Farm is engaged in the business of storing oil products. With significant operations in the Baltic Sea and all terminals in Rotterdam and Amsterdam through our joint terminal venture with LBC Tank Terminals, our company is headquartered in Rotterdam. The Transport Ministry Department of Sea and River Transport of Rotterdam has granted our company all necessary licenses so that it may do its business on Dutch inland waterways as well as at sea and at foreign sea ports.
