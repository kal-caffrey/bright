Cargo Handling

We are Russia's No 1 in handling all sizes and types of Cargoes

With the help of contemporary equipment and our knowledgeable, experienced staff, we can better assist you.

The most effective loading, unloading, receiving, and delivery of cargoes are the responsibilities of 1/2 LOGISTICS BV. under a Concession Contract with the major Ports Authority in Rotterdam. The handling of shipments by our Cargo Department includes procurement, storage and warehousing, transportation, and pricing risk management. Neither a big nor a small consumer is beyond our ability to serve. We take pleasure in providing all of our customers with superior cargo handling services.
